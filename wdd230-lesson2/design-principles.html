Rule of Thirds

Adamas

Adamas.com

>

In this website, the user can really see how the rule of thirds has been used to draw their attention into the subject matter. Ideally, the empty space that's left should be in the direction of the subject is looking or heading into. This website best illustrates this design principle.

Hick's Law

Soap2Day

Soap2Day.com

>

Hick's law states that the more the stimuli/choices users face, the longer it will take them to make a decision. Hick's law acknowledges the precious time of the user by categorizing the choice to help users locate their options as fast as possible. This is basically obscuring complexity. This movie website illustrates just this design concept.
